WHY I LOVE MD

Family

I love Maryland because that is where my family is. They are the most loving and suppotive people I know, and as long as they are there, Maryland will always be my favorite state.

2. Friends

My friends have been there through so much with me. I depend on them when I am down, and they never disappoint me. I love my friends who are now like my fam! These people make living in Maryland fun and exciting. Just when you think you've exhausted all ideas for fun things to do, my friends find some way to make even the most boring activities fun.

3. Crabs

Maryland crabs are lit!!!! We steam them, then smother them thangs in hella old bay seasoning! Crabs are a huge part of Maryland culture. At times, entire events are planned around crabs. Come get you some!

FACTS

Baltimore Ravens

The Baltimore Ravens are named after author Edgar Allen Poe's famous poem, The Raven.

Official Sport

Lacrosse became the official team sport of Maryland in 2004.

Known for its clean beaches and feral ponies, Assateague Island is visited by over 2.1 million people annually.

Maryland is the national leader in the production of blue crabs and soft clams. Marylanders are known for dousing their crabs in copious amounts of Old Bay seasoning.
